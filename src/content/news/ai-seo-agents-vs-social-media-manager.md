There is nothing wrong with hiring a social media manager if the goal is social distribution.

But if the goal is **search traffic**, the comparison changes.

Search rewards durable assets:

- landing pages
- comparison pages
- support articles
- freshness updates

That is why AI SEO agents are often the better place to put the budget.

## What a social media manager produces

A social media manager can keep the feed active.

That can be useful.

But a lot of that work is rented attention. Once the post is gone, the asset is gone.

## What AI SEO agents produce

AI SEO agents can help produce assets that stay on the site:

- new pages
- updated pages
- stronger content clusters
- better technical follow-through

Those assets keep working after they are published.

## Why this matters for lean teams

If you do not have budget for both, you have to ask where the compounding happens.

For many businesses, it happens in search.

That is why this comparison matters so much.
