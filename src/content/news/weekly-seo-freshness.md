Search growth gets sold like a campaign.

In practice, it behaves more like maintenance.

That is why **weekly freshness** matters.

Freshness is not random rewriting. It is the habit of making the site more current, more complete, and more connected every single week.

## What a weekly freshness pass looks like

A real freshness pass can be small:

- update a title
- add a missing section
- improve internal links
- add examples that make the page stronger
- tighten the call to action

None of those tasks is dramatic on its own. Together they keep the site from going stale.

## Why this beats one giant quarterly push

The quarterly push sounds strategic, but it usually means the site sits still for too long.

Weekly updates win because:

- they are easier to sustain
- they create more iteration
- they compound faster
- they are less likely to get skipped entirely

## Why AI agents are useful here

AI agents do not get bored by repetitive SEO work.

That is exactly why they are useful.

They can help identify which pages need attention, draft the update ideas, and keep the queue from vanishing into someone's notes app.
