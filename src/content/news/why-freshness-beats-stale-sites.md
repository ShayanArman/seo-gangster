There are sites that look strong on day one and then slowly stop moving.

There are other sites that start simpler but keep getting better every week.

The second kind often wins.

That is why freshness matters so much.

## Why stale sites lose

Stale sites lose because they stop signaling effort.

They stop getting new supporting content.

They stop getting updates.

They stop improving their own structure.

Eventually the difference shows.

## Why small updates are powerful

A freshness update does not have to be massive to matter.

Sometimes the real win is:

- a stronger heading
- a clearer explanation
- a new comparison section
- a few internal links
- a better CTA

Those small edits keep the page alive.
