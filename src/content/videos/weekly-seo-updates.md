Most teams understand the first half of SEO: build the site and publish the first set of articles.

This video is about the second half: keeping those pages fresh enough that they keep compounding.

## What this video covers

- Why weekly updates outperform long gaps between changes
- How freshness turns old pages back into growth assets
- Why AI agents make this cadence easier to sustain

## Why this matters

The site that keeps improving usually beats the site that launched bigger and then went quiet.
