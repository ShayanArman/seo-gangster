Most sites do not lose because the first launch was impossible.

They lose because the site stops moving after launch.

That is the philosophy behind SEO Gangster. Search growth needs a system for page creation, article support, and weekly freshness.

## The idea behind the video

- SEO is ongoing work, not a one-time build
- AI agents help with the repetitive execution layer
- Freshness matters because search rewards sites that keep improving

## Why this matters

If the site does not keep changing, it eventually starts fading in search.
